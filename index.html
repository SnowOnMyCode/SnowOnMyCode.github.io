Memristive SNN — Online STDP learning of A + B

Two Poisson inputs A, B drive a single LIF output through Biolek HP memristor synapses. Pair-based STDP applies voltage pulses to the memristors; teacher forcing drives the output at rate A + B so the synapses shape toward summation. Simulation runs entirely in your browser (no server).
